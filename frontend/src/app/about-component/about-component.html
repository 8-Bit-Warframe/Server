What are 8-Bit Warframe and Lost Sector?

Lost Sector is a retro action/RPG/platformer, set in the Warframe universe. It is the first game being produced as part of the "8-Bit Warframe project".

Who is making Lost Sector?

Lost Sector is being worked on by a multitude of people, all of whom are part of the Warframe community. Nearly 100 people have now contributed directly to its development!
